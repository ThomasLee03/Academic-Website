About SpongeBob SquarePants

Biographical Information

SpongeBob SquarePants is a fun-loving, optimistic sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, where his incredible cooking skills make him one of the best fry cooks in Bikini Bottom. Despite his frequent mishaps, SpongeBob always remains cheerful and enjoys spending time with his best friend Patrick Star, and his pet snail, Gary.

He was created by marine science educator and animator Stephen Hillenburg and made his television debut on May 1, 1999. SpongeBob has since become one of the most iconic and beloved animated characters worldwide, known for his infectious laugh and childlike enthusiasm.
